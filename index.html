About

Built from the ground up and managed by owners who have been in the laundromat industry for over 20 years, we take pride in delivering consistent, reliable, and professional laundry services. We offer a modern experience with convenient laundry cards, free Wi-Fi, and a spacious environment. Whether you need self-service, drop-off laundry, or dry cleaning, we're here to help.

Services

Drop-off Laundry

Our drop-off service is priced per pound, with an optional white-separate wash available for an additional $1. Please note that blankets, comforters, and pillows are priced individually and not by weight. Enjoy same-day pickup when you dropped off before noon.

Dry Cleaning

Dry clean garments are priced per item. For specific pricing details, please call or visit us.

Self-service

Our laundromat is open 24/7 for your convenience, featuring a wide selection of washers and dryers to suit your needs. We use a refillable laundry card for a modern and coin-free experience.

Please note that all prices and services are subject to change without notice.
